About the Workshop

This workshop focuses on the intersection of machine learning and network systems. We aim to bring together researchers to discuss how autonomous networks can scale to meet the demands of 2026 and beyond.

Call for Papers

We invite submissions of original, unpublished work (up to 6 pages). Topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Self-driving networks and automated management
  • In-network computing and offloading
  • Privacy-preserving telemetry
