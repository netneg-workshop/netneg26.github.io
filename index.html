Call for Papers

The [Workshop Name] seeks to bring together researchers and practitioners to discuss the latest advancements in [Topic]. As [Topic] becomes increasingly critical for [Industry/Research], we face new challenges in performance, security, and scalability.

We welcome contributions including, but not limited to, the following topics:

  • Innovative approaches to [Topic A]
  • Case studies and real-world deployments
  • Formal verification and safety of [Topic B]
  • Performance optimization techniques

Submission Instructions

We are looking for two types of submissions:

  • Research papers: Up to 6 pages (excluding references).
  • Poster abstracts: Up to 2 pages, showcasing early-stage findings.

Submissions must be original work and follow the ACM SIGCOMM formatting rules. All submissions are double-blind.

Please submit your papers via HotCRP.
