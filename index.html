Overview

The Workshop on Example Systems (WES 2026) brings together researchers and practitioners interested in operating systems, networking, and systems programming.

The workshop will be co-located with a major systems conference.

Call for Papers

We invite submissions describing original research, experience reports, and position papers related to the workshop topics.

Topics of Interest

  • Systems performance and optimization
  • Kernel extensions and programmability
  • Networking and observability
  • Security and verification
  • Production deployments and case studies

Submission Guidelines

  • Research papers: up to 6 pages
  • Poster abstracts: up to 2 pages
  • Double-blind review
