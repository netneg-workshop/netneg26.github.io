The Workshop on Example Systems (WES 2026) brings together researchers and practitioners interested in modern systems, networking, and infrastructure.

About

This workshop aims to foster discussion on emerging ideas and practical deployments in modern computing systems. Participants will present research, share experiences, and collaborate on future directions.

Call for Papers

We invite submissions describing original research, experience reports, and position papers related to the workshop topics.

  • Kernel extensions and programmability
  • Networking and observability
  • Security and verification
  • Production deployments and case studies
